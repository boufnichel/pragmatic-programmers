Pragmatic Programmers SARL-AU is a software development company based in Rabat, Morocco, specializing in mobile apps, digital products, AI-powered utilities, backend engineering, and cloud-native architectures.

## About the Company
We are a focused engineering partner delivering secure, compliant, and resilient digital solutions. Our teams combine enterprise-grade rigor with the adaptability of modern product studios, ensuring every engagement meets the expectations of demanding app marketplaces and regulated industries.

## What We Do
- **Mobile Experiences:** Native iOS, Android, and cross-platform solutions with robust CI/CD pipelines.
- **Digital Products:** End-to-end delivery of consumer and enterprise applications, from research to launch.
- **AI-Powered Utilities:** Applied machine learning, data engineering, and responsible AI integrations.
- **Backend Engineering:** API platforms, event-driven systems, and integrations with critical business systems.
- **Cloud-Native Architectures:** Secure, scalable deployments across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
